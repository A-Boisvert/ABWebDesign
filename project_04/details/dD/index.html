Crowd Control at DEFCON Music Festival

Link to the video.

This is a shot of the crowd at DEFCON 2018, a music festival dedicated to the high-energy genre of Hardstyle.

They have grown a custom of setting records of "Crowd Control", where the performers guide the audience to grab one another's shoulders and take eight steps left then right.

The trend caught on throughout festivals in 2017 among DJs as a chance to create some awesome crowd interaction and wonderful video shots, however none could do it quite as well as DEFCON.

I idealize creating some kind of accompaniment to a Festival live set where I could get the crowd as involved as this.
