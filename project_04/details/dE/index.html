Link to the video.

Created by Yasutaka Nakata x Kamikaze Douga, this concept video was made for the upcoming mobile game from the "Tales Of" franchise, a game series dear to my heart!

The animation is not purely made as pixel art, it is a filter effect over a contemporary 3D animation, but the style of it is charming and some of the action scenes are quite smooth. I would love to work on something like this.
