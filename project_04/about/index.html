This is a collection of Animations that stick with me, not necessarily in any order.

I wanted to review my Favorites playlist on Youtube for impressive, thoughtful, or just mesmerizing movement and showcase them all together with the ability to control the movement.

I study Game Design and mess with mathematical patterns, Shaders, and animation - many of these animations influence my creative process and stand as the kinds of things I want to make.
